HOW TO MAKE THE MOST OF SOCIAL MEDIA.

Some other social media that you may effectively employ to share your Projectlive campaign include: Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat, LinkedIn, and Snapchat.

Instagram: to share your Projectlive campaign on Instagram: - take pictures documenting your campaign journey - take pictures of events relating to your campaign - tag your pictures with a link to your campaign - tag words that relate to your campaign - follow people on Instagram who may be interested in your project or cause - use photos to show your campaign, rewards and more.

Instagram does not allow clickable URLs in posts; as such you have to add your campaign link to your Instagram bio so that your friends, family and community know how to find your campaign. Then refer to the link in your bio in your posts. You may go the extra mile of creating an Instagram account or a hashtag particularly for your Projectlive campaign.

 

This way your supporters can support your project by sharing your hashtag; and people would be able to easily find your campaign across all social media channels with a short phrase. Importantly, you should post at the times when your social networks are actively online. Using Instagram to share your Projectlive campaign can be an effective way to get support from people who are more inclined to use this media to network and discover.

 

Twitter: if you choose to share your campaign via Twitter: Be sure to pick a predetermined hashtag to rally your posts around and include it in each of your tweet. - use popular and trending hashtags to raise awareness for your crowdfunding campaign - always include your campaign link whenever you tweet about it - ask for re-tweets to help spread the word - tweet at people who might have an interest in your campaign - gain followers by following others and actively engaging them - be careful not to tweet too much to avoid spam - ask your friends and inner circle to tweet to their followers about the campaign


Post updates about the progress of your campaign frequently. This will keep donors and visitors to your page interested and encouraged particularly when they can see that you are indeed making progress. But you must be honest about your progress/status report. Posting frequent update is one way to continue engaging the people who want to help you most. You may share updates on how donations are being used; "thank you" shout outs to those who have donated or shared; and how close you are to your goal. You may include a call to action like: "Thanks for reading. Please donate and spread the word to a few people you would like to share my story with".

 

Show your appreciation. Regularly say thank you to your donors for giving you their hard earned money to help you accomplish something important to you. Your show of gratitude will warm the hearts of your donors and make them feel satisfied for taking part in an act of selfless service.
